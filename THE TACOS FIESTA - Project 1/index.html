The Taco Trivia

When did the Tacos first appeared in India ?

Tacos, as part of Mexican cuisine, began gaining popularity in India in the early 2000s, influenced by global food trends and the rise of international restaurant chains. The introduction of Taco Bell in Bangalore in 2010 significantly boosted the accessibility and popularity of tacos across the country. Before this, some high-end restaurants in metropolitan areas might have offered Mexican dishes, but it was the entry of chain restaurants that made tacos more mainstream in India.(Source: Smithsonian Magazine )
